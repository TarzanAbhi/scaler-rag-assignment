Placement Policy - Scaler Academy

1. Placement Guarantee: Scaler guarantees placement for all eligible 
   graduates within 6 months of program completion.

2. Eligibility for Placement: Students must maintain 80% attendance 
   and complete all assignments to be eligible for placement support.

3. Placement Process: Scaler connects students with 900+ hiring partners 
   including Google, Amazon, Microsoft, Flipkart, and startups.

4. Average Package: The average CTC offered to Scaler graduates is 18 LPA.

5. If Not Placed: If a student is not placed within 6 months despite 
   meeting eligibility criteria, Scaler will refund the full program fee.

6. Placement Support: Includes resume reviews, mock interviews, 
   and direct referrals to hiring partners.
