Refund and Cancellation Policy - Scaler Academy

1. Full Refund: Students are eligible for a full refund if they cancel 
   within 7 days of enrollment.

2. Partial Refund: A 50% refund is available if cancellation is made 
   between 7 and 30 days of enrollment.

3. No Refund: No refund is provided after 30 days of enrollment.

4. Placement Refund: If Scaler fails to place you within 6 months of 
   program completion, a full fee refund is processed within 45 days.

5. EMI Cancellation: For EMI plans, cancellation stops future debits 
   but past EMIs are non-refundable.
