Eligibility Criteria - Scaler Academy

1. Educational Qualification: Bachelor's degree in any discipline.

2. Work Experience: Minimum 1 year of professional software development 
   experience required.

3. Programming Knowledge: Basic knowledge of at least one programming 
   language (Python, Java, C++, or JavaScript).

4. Age: No age limit for applying to Scaler Academy.

5. Selection Process: Applicants must clear a technical screening test 
   and an interview before enrollment.

6. Students in final year of graduation are also eligible to apply.
