Frequently Asked Questions - Scaler Academy

Q: What is the duration of the program?
A: The Scaler Academy program is 9 months long, conducted on weekends.

Q: What programming languages are taught?
A: We teach Python, Java, C++, Data Structures, Algorithms, and System Design.

Q: Is there a placement guarantee?
A: Yes, Scaler offers a placement guarantee. If you don't get placed within 
6 months of graduation, we refund your full fee.

Q: What is the minimum salary offered?
A: The average salary offered to Scaler graduates is 18 LPA, with top 
packages going up to 50 LPA.

Q: Who can apply to Scaler?
A: Working professionals with at least 1 year of experience in software 
development can apply.
